ROYDON MISSELDINE

I’m a Wellington based graphic designer currently working at Strategy Creative.

Focusing on solid conceptual foundations I aim to create work that is transparent and vibrant. This approach has enabled me to work effortlessly across disciplines and help breathe new life into brands.

If you have a project or collaboration in mind, please get in touch.
